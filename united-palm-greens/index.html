About the Project

A Community
Built for Living

United Palm Greens is a landmark gated residential community developed by the prestigious Al Waheed Group. Thoughtfully designed for families who value security, serenity, and a premium quality of life, this development sets a new standard for community living in Karachi. Lush landscaping, wide palm-lined boulevards, and world-class amenities define life within United Palm Greens. Every detail, from the grand entrance gate to the internal road network, has been designed to create an environment of peace, exclusivity, and long-term value.

"A rare opportunity to own in one of Karachi's most anticipated gated communities, exclusively marketed by HK Builders & Developers."
