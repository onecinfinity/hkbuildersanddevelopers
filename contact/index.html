Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Do you charge any fee for consultation?+
No. All consultations, site visits, and initial discussions with HK Builders & Developers are completely free. We only assist you in connecting with the right development for your needs.
Can overseas Pakistanis invest remotely?+
Yes, absolutely. We have a dedicated team for overseas clients who can manage the entire process remotely, from documentation to power of attorney arrangements, with full transparency at every step.
How do I book a site visit?+
Simply call us, message us on WhatsApp, or fill out the enquiry form on any project page. Our team will arrange a convenient time for your visit and accompany you throughout.
Are the payment plans flexible?+
Most of the projects we market offer easy installment plans tailored to different budgets. Our consultants can walk you through the specific plans available for each development.
Is HK Builders the developer of these projects?+
No, HK Builders & Developers is a real estate marketing partner. We market projects on behalf of credible developers like Al Waheed Group and Falaknaz Group, providing our clients with expert guidance and support throughout their journey.
